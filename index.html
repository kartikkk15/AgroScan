About AgroScan

AgroScan is designed to help farmers connect directly with consumers through QR codes. By scanning the QR code, buyers can access detailed product information, including origin, price, and availability. Our mission is to create a transparent and efficient marketplace for agricultural products.

This project is developed by 2nd-year AIML students of Ramdeobaba College of Engineering and Management as part of a Community Engagement initiative.
